About PowerFit Gym

Transforming lives through fitness since 2010

Our Story

From Passion to Purpose

Founded in 2010 by fitness enthusiasts Sarah and Mike Johnson, PowerFit Gym started as a small neighborhood fitness center with a big vision. We believed that everyone deserves access to quality fitness facilities and professional guidance.

Over the years, we've grown into one of the city's premier fitness destinations, but our core values remain the same: community, excellence, and transformation.

Today, we serve over 5,000 members with state-of-the-art equipment, diverse classes, and a supportive community that feels like family.
